  <p class="cv-lede">Selected research and software projects across long-read sequencing, clinical cohort informatics, host-pathogen genomics, microbial annotation, and immunoinformatics.</p>

<section class="project-grid">
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">CSIR-IGIB</p><h2>ONT Dengue Genomic Surveillance</h2><p>End-to-end analysis of Oxford Nanopore dengue sequencing data across longitudinal clinical cohorts, integrating basecalling, alignment, variant calling, ARTIC workflows, and downstream cohort interpretation.</p></article>
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">Clinical metadata automation</p><h2>Maxscriber</h2><p>Linux CLI tool automating clinical metadata transcription, patient stratification, QC validation, and statistical visualization for ONT dengue surveillance projects.</p></article>
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">Epigenomics</p><h2>Haplotype-Resolved Methylation Profiling</h2><p>Long-read human whole-genome sequencing workflow using Modkit for phased CpG methylation analysis, differential methylation profiling, and gene-level epigenetic annotation.</p></article>
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">Pathogen genomics</p><h2>ESKAPE Genomic Characterization</h2><p>Ongoing SNP-based characterization of ESKAPE pathogens from ONT data across national and international collaborative infectious disease projects.</p></article>
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">M.Sc. dissertation</p><h2>HBV Multi-Epitope Vaccine Design</h2><p>Reverse vaccinology workflow that screened more than 111,000 HBV protein sequences across 8 genotypes and produced a structurally validated multi-epitope construct.</p></article>
  <article><p class="cv-kicker">CSIR-IMTECH</p><h2>Microbial Genome Annotation Benchmarking</h2><p>Comparative benchmarking of five prokaryotic annotation pipelines on Thermodesulfobium narugense, including structure-assisted validation of a missed Signal Peptidase II gene.</p></article>
</section>
